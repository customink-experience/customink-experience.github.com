Quoting a price for your group
X  Close
Worried about collecting the right amount of money to cover the actual cost of your order?  Here's the top 4 things you can do to make sure you're covered:

1. Remember personalized names and numbers. If you need to print individual names and/or numbers on your design, call us so we can give you an accurate quote.

2. Use a reasonable minimum and maximum quantity for your quote.  You can show your group a price range, an approximate amount, or don't show a price on the sign-up sheet if you are not sure.

   Examples:  "About $15", "$12-15 each  "$12 for 30, $15 for 10"

3. Round the price up or add an extra dollar.  If you have funds left over after your final order, you can give a refund or hold the funds for your group to use for their next order.

4. Order in time to use Standard Delivery for FREE SHIPPING.  We offer FREE SHIPPING to the 48 US States and Canada.  Need delivery sooner or live outside of our free shipping area?  Give us a call for pricing.
