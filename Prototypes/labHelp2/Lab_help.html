To adjust amount the "scrolling content panel" in the scroll bar zones:
- move the panel up until the content fits as you like it
- CTRL-L and note the "Top" value
- divide Top by 4 (or one less than the number of zones on your scroll bar) to get an "increment" value
- leave zone 1 at 0,0
- set zone 2 to (0,increment)
- set zone 3 to (0,incrementx2)
- set zone 4 to (0,incrementx3)
- set zone 5 to (0,incrementx4), which is also (0,Top)
