Help Center
What's the minimum required for an order?

The minimum quantity varies by the product you select.  Some of our most popular shirts are available with no minimum, although the color selection may be limited for quantities below 6.  Products with embroidery require a minimum of 12. 

Please check the minimum quantity information in the product catalog for more information.
How can I reduce the cost of my order?

CustomInk's pricing for each order is based on the product, quantity, printing method, number of ink colors (for screen printing), and one sided vs. two sided printing.  All of our price quotes are all-inclusive with no hidden costs or fees.  

There are many things that factor into your price:  product, quantity, printing method, number of colors used, number of printing areas.  Here are some suggestions for reducing your cost:

1) Select a lower cost product.  Look for a single $ in the product catalog to find those that are "budget friendly."  Order with confidence knowing that all CustomInk products are quality tested and have a money back guarantee.

2) Decrease the print colors.  Reducing colors for screen printed images or changing full color photographs to single color can greatly reduce your cost.  If you are ordering 6 or more shirts and want to include an image with a high number of colors, our expert artists will work with you to recreate your design to increase print clarity and reduce the number of colors.  This is a FREE service. 

3) Print on one side.  Personalizing shirts with individual names and numbers adds to your cost.

4) Add our logo to your design.  Save .50 per shirts on average when you add our logo. 

5) Use standard shipping.  It's FREE to the 48 continental United States.

6) Contact us!  Our friendly CustomInk Service agents will work with you to make your design idea into reality at a price you can afford.
