How is my price determined?

CustomInk's pricing for each order is based on mixture of the following:

- product selection
- quantity
- decoration method (digital printing, screen printing, embroidery)
- number of ink colors in your design (for screen printing)
- areas used for printing (one side, two side, sleeve, hood, leg)
- personalization (adding individual names and numbers)
.
