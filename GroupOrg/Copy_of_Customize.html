What factors affect my order price?

Quantity  Generally, the more items you order at one time, the lower the cost.  Remember to order extras for appreciation gifts or to have o n hand for late-comers.  You'll save money by ordering a single large quantity rather than several smaller ones.

Personalized Names and Numbers: If your order will include individual names or numbers, give us a call to get a complete quote to include these options.

Product Selection: Standard, short-sleeve T-shirts cost less than premium styles like sweatshirts or long-sleeve T-shirts. Try the Gildan Ultra Cotton T for a great value option.  White garments are the most economical color choice.  It's like painting over a wall in your house - you need more ink to cover a dark product than a white one.

Printing: Printing in one location, either the front or back of the item, and using a single color design is the most economical.

Delivery:  We offer Free Shipping with 2-week delivery to the 48 continental United Staes and Canada.  If you need a faster delivery or need delivery outside of the 48 states, please call us for an accurate price.

Other factors: If you need items in plus sizes or a separate, smaller print of your design for youth sizes, please call us for amn accurate quote.
Here are the top 3 strategies that group organizers tell us they use:

1. Get an exact quote.  Be sure to include the quantity and options you'll need in your final order.  See "Top Factors that Affect Your Price" at the bottom of this page or call us if you need more information.

2. Round the price up or add an extra dollar.  If you have funds left over after your final order, you can give a refund or hold the funds for your group to use for their next order.

3. Show a price range.  If you are unsure of the quantity you'll need, get a quote for the smallest and largest quantity for your pricing.
